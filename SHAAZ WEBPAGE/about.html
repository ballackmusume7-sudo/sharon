About the University of Kabianga

Founded in 2005, the University of Kabianga has grown into a beacon of academic excellence and innovation. Located in the heart of Kenya, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs aimed at equipping students with the knowledge and skills required to thrive in a globalized world. With state-of-the-art facilities, highly qualified faculty, and a commitment to research and development, we are shaping the future of education.

Our Mission and Values

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible, and innovative education that fosters intellectual curiosity, research, and leadership, while promoting sustainable development and societal transformation.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Diversity, Sustainability, and Collaboration.

Our Vision

To be a world-class institution committed to producing globally competitive and socially responsible graduates.
